Melissa Odoi

Passionate UI UX Designer

My UX Skills

User Research

I conduct user research to understand what users need and how they behave. By gathering insights through interviews, surveys, and testing, I make informed design choices that solve real problems and create better user experiences.

User Interface Design

I specialize in creating visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. My focus is on crafting clean, responsive designs that make navigation seamless and interactions effortless. From wireframes to final visual elements, I ensure that every component serves a purpose while maintaining a modern and user-friendly aesthetic.

Web Development

I create clean, user-friendly websites with a focus on responsive design and easy navigation. My goal is to make websites visually appealing and functional, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.

My Work

Fish and Wildlife Service Website Redesign

As part of a team project, we redesigned the Fish and Wildlife Service website to create a more engaging, intuitive experience for users. Our goal was to make important conservation information and resources easier to access, with a clean and structured layout. By improving navigation, adding eye-catching visuals, and focusing on usability, our redesign helps users explore wildlife initiatives, learn about protected species, and get involved in conservation efforts more easily.

The Recipe Website Redesign

My group and I designed and developed The Recipe website from the ground up to offer users a sleek, intuitive space to explore and save their favorite recipes. With a modern, dark aesthetic, the site is built to be both visually appealing and easy to navigate. Users can upload their own recipes or find new ones to suit their taste, creating a personalized digital recipe book that’s timeless and effortless to use.

D.A.R.E. Website Redesign

For this project, me and my team redesigned the D.A.R.E. website to create a more user-friendly and engaging experience. My focus was on making information easily accessible while modernizing the look to appeal to a younger audience. Through clean layouts, bright visuals, and organized content, the new design helps users quickly find resources and stay connected to D.A.R.E.’s mission of drug awareness and prevention.
